About Me

Hi! My name is Andrew Cooke.

I am an undergrad at Brown University studying computer science. My concentration focuses on software engineering, machine learning/AI, and game development. I am part of the Brown Men's Crew Team and have competed at the Head of the Charles and the IRA National Championship.

In my free time I love to draw, learn guitar and piano, work on graphic design, write, and go for long walks!

Projects

2D Game Engine

Over the course of one semester, I designed and implemented the architecture for a 2D game engine. It's framework is made up of user interface elements, game objects, and components. It supports collisions, configurable physics, AI scriptings, animations, and much more!

I have used my engine to design several 2D games including a platformer and procedurally generated dungeon crawler.

Chess Board Recognition

Implemented a computer vision program using deep learning to recognize the position of a chess board from a given image and convert it to a textual representation.
